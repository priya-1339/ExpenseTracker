About

Personal Expense Tracker

Track your daily expenses easily with this simple and intuitive expense tracker. Manage your spending across different categories, view visual summaries, and gain insights into your financial habits.

Features:
  • Add, edit, and delete expenses
  • Categorize expenses for better organization
  • Filter expenses by category and date range
  • Visual charts showing spending patterns
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go tracking
  • Install as an app on your phone (PWA)

Built with Flask, SQLite, and Bootstrap for a fast and reliable experience.
